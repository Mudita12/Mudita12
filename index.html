Workflow

A collaborative process from discovery to handoff.

Clear checkpoints keep product, business, and user goals aligned through each iteration.

  1. Discover

    Understand users, constraints, and measurable success goals.

    Interviews Audits Competitor scan

  2. Define

    Turn insights into user flows and information architecture.

    Personas Journey maps IA

  3. Design

    Move from wireframes to high-fidelity screens and prototypes.

  4. Deliver
